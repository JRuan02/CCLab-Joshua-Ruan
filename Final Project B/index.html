Piano Nursery Rhymes

For this project, I wanted to create a fun an easy way to play the piano. As a kid, I would occasionally to try to copy the piano tile videos online without actually learning how to play. So for this project, I wanted to create an easy and fun interative piano with 9 keys. On the next page, follow the numbers in order and play some of the most famous nursery rhymes.
